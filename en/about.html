About Us

Strategic Technological Ally

At Ingeniería Avante de Occidente (IAO), we are a Mexican company specializing in the design, engineering, manufacturing, and installation of comprehensive industrial solutions aimed at maximizing the efficiency, safety, and operational continuity of our clients.

With +20 years of experience, we have consolidated ourselves as a strategic technological ally for high-demand industries, developing turnkey projects ranging from conceptual and detailed engineering to certified manufacturing, civil works, assembly, and industrial maintenance.

We are masters in Industrial Engineering and Manufacturing under ASME standards, allowing us to guarantee reliable, safe equipment aligned with international regulations.

Specialties

Our Specialty

We serve sectors such as sugar, paper, tequila, dairy, energy, and general industry, offering solutions tailored to each process.

ASME Certified Manufacturing

We design and manufacture pressure vessels, heat exchangers, tanks, and process equipment, ensuring maximum safety, durability, and international regulatory compliance. Our specialty lies in detail engineering applied to welding and heavy fabrication under the strictest standards.

End-to-End Integral Service
